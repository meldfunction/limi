What we do

Not consulting. Not coaching.
Accompaniment.

Limicelia occupies a distinct space in the organizational development field. We don't arrive with frameworks to impose or benchmarks to chase. We arrive with rigorous presence — the capacity to be fully with an organization as it moves through the liminal thresholds that matter most.

Our name is drawn from the biology of threshold crossings — those moments when organisms traverse membranes, navigate edges, move between states. We believe organizations face genuine thresholds, not just problems, and that they deserve partners equipped to accompany them through.

We work closely with founding teams, evolving co-leadership structures, mission-driven organizations entering new chapters, and institutions confronting identity questions at depth.

How we work

03

We follow the organization's own knowing.

Every organization holds within it an intelligence about its own situation — often obscured, rarely absent. Our work begins with deep listening before any diagnosis, and continues by helping leadership access and trust what they already carry. We are not the experts on your organization. You are.

We name what is actually happening.

Organizational difficulty is often not a strategy problem. It is a relational, structural, or meaning problem that has been translated into strategy language because that language feels safer. We are trained to work at the level where the actual difficulty lives — and to do so without creating new harm.

We work at the pace transformation requires.

We do not accelerate processes that need time. Real change in human systems has its own rhythm. We hold space for that rhythm, protecting against the urgency that often produces the very fragility organizations are trying to escape. Our engagements are scoped accordingly.
