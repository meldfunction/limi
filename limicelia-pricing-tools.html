About Monthly Retainer

A monthly retainer provides predictable cash flow for Limicelia and clear budgeting for the client. The client commits to a monthly fee for the duration of the engagement. Best for transformation and anchor partner engagements where the scope evolves month to month. Recommended for most Limicelia engagements.
