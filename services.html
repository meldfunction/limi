Limicelia · Services

Seven domains.
Custom every time.

We don't offer a service menu. We build custom engagements at the intersection of domains — all following the same arc: listen from the frontline up, name what the system produces, stay through the hard part, leave capacity — not a report.

01 / Practice domains

The seven
service domains.

Most engagements draw from several simultaneously. If you know something is broken but aren't sure what it needs — that is exactly where we start.

Organizational Design & Governance

6–9 months

Sociocracy · Consent-Based Decision-Making · Role Clarity

Sociocratic and participatory structures, decision-making frameworks, role clarity and accountability systems that reflect how the organization actually wants to operate — not how the org chart says it should.

Leadership & Culture Transformation

6–9 months

Inner-Outer Integration · Culture Change · Relational Repair

Working with leaders and teams through inner and outer work simultaneously — shifting culture, healing relational dynamics, building conditions for sustained change without burning out the people doing it.

Community Engagement & Participatory Design

4–6 months

Frontline Intelligence · Co-Design · Not Consultation Theater

Bringing community intelligence into the center of strategy, service, and product — not as consultation theater but as actual co-design where what the community knows shapes what gets built.

Conflict Resolution & Coalition Building

3–6 months

Deep Democracy · Power-Aware · Multi-Stakeholder

Facilitated processes for navigating conflict, cross-sector tension, and coalition formation where power differentials are real and need to be named, not managed.

Field Building & Ecosystem Convening

Per convening or ongoing

Warm Data Labs · Open Space · Systems Convening

Designing and hosting convenings that strengthen connective tissue between practitioners, funders, researchers, and institutions at field edges. Not networking events — encounters that change how people think.

Organizational Memory & Knowledge

3–4 months

Harvest · Transition Support · Living Documents

Capturing, stewarding, and activating institutional knowledge — especially in leadership transitions where what was learned must not leave with the person who's leaving.

Technology & AI Strategy

3–6 months

North Star · Organizational Imagination · Regenerative Adoption

AI transformation that creates capacity for meaningful work rather than workload creep and burnout. Community-led product development, knowledge management, data sovereignty.

02 / Engagement types

Three ways
to enter.

Most engagements are custom — built at the intersection of several domains. We start with a diagnostic conversation, not a service selection.

Diagnostic · 3 months

What's actually broken

Discovery + deep listening + honest diagnosis. Deliverable: diagnostic report with recommended pathways. For organizations who know something's wrong but aren't sure what it needs.

Transformation · 6 months

Changing how you work

Full six-phase arc: discovery through harvest. Redesigned structures, practices, and capacity that the organization owns after we leave.

Anchor Partner · 12+ months

Sustained presence

Embedded practitioner through multiple transitions. For organizations navigating continuous transformation, meta-organizations, and complex systems that need a sustained thinking partner.

If you know what's broken but not what it needs, that's the right place to begin. The diagnostic conversation is not a sales call — it is genuine inquiry into what the situation is asking for.
