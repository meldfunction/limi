Purpose & Vision
01

The work is better because
the ecosystem is healthy.

Most consulting firms burn through practitioners the same way their clients burn through staff. High turnover. Junior people doing senior work. No one staying long enough to understand what's actually happening. You feel it — even when you can't name it.

Every practitioner in our network is developed through years of shared practice, peer supervision, and real engagements. They stay because the model sustains them. When you hire Limicelia, you get practitioners who are sharp, rested, and backed by a deep bench. That's not a side effect. It's the design.

We listen from the frontline up, name what the system is actually producing, and stay through the hard part. The people closest to the problem hold the most accurate picture of it. We build from there.

Our Model
03

Where your
investment goes.

Fees from well-resourced clients fund the full ecosystem: practitioner development, peer supervision, open-source tools, and access for organizations that can't pay. Surplus becomes access, not retained earnings.

We are post-growth by design — 15 practitioners, 30 clients, enough defined before revenue starts. Every dollar you spend strengthens the network that shows up for your engagement.
