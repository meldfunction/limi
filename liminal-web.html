What It Is
01

Not a roster.
A web of relationships.

The Liminal Web is the connective tissue of Limicelia's work — a living network of practitioners who inhabit threshold spaces between disciplines, between sectors, between what is and what wants to emerge.

Liminal: because most important work happens in the between — in transitions, threshold moments, the space where old forms are composting and new ones haven't yet solidified. Web: because mycelial logic governs here — distributed, resilient, no single center, nutrients flowing where needed, strength emerging from connection not hierarchy.

Not a contractor pool. Not a vendor list. Not a certification program. When you work with Limicelia, you enter this web of relationships — practitioners who share practice, accountability, and the patient work of organizational restoration. Trust is built inside the web before any client engagement happens. That's not gatekeeping. It's how quality gets protected without hierarchy.

Structure
02

Four concentric
circles of participation.

Core Stewards · 2–3
$120k draw + profit share
Hold the web, steward relationships, protect values under market pressure. Full-time. No exit incentive — ownership is stewardship. Changed by every engagement.
Active Practitioners · 5–8
100% of agreed hourly rate
Regularly deployed. Governance voice. Co-own the methodology, shape the commons. 1–3 engagements per year. Full participants in the web — not contractors called in for projects.
Specialist Associates · 8–12
Craft-specific depth
Specific expertise called in for particular engagements. 1–2 per year. Contribute to commons without full governance. The web's depth where depth is needed.
Collaborator Pool · 5–10
Doorway into deeper membership
Occasional, project-specific participation. First engagements. Testing fit in both directions. No pressure to go deeper; some become Active Practitioners over time.
Development
03

Five tiers of
practitioner development.

Practitioners grow through a living development ecosystem, not training programs. Each tier builds the ground the next requires. The progression is not certification — it is accumulating the actual capacities that hard organizational work demands.

A
Being & Presence
Starter Cultures circles. Free. Practitioner's own ground — capacity to stay present in difficulty. Not soft work. Prerequisite for everything else.
B
Relational Craft
Intentional Society + Starter Cultures. Minimal fee. Being present with another's reality without merging, distancing, or managing. T-Group, Circling, Bohm Dialogue.
C
Organizational Craft
Starter Cultures, Limicelia-sponsored. Specific methods mapped to service domains. Sociocracy Sandbox, Facilitation Dojo, Conflict Navigation Practice.
D
Wisdom Traditions
Curated by Limicelia. Woven throughout all tiers. Epistemological ground — the wells practitioners draw from, not frameworks they apply.
Tier E: Liminal Web Integration Invitation, not graduation. Practitioners become visible through their practice in Starter Cultures and Intentional Society. Trust builds before any first engagement. No application. No interview. Known through practice before invited.
Ecosystem
04

The web within
the wider field.

The Liminal Web doesn't exist in isolation. It sits inside a network of organizations that share aligned practice and values — each playing a different role in the ecosystem Limicelia is trying to help grow.

Present of Work — Field context and values. A translocal network and space of inquiry for post-growth alternatives to extractive work systems. No entity; pure field.

Starter Cultures — Inner cultivation home. Free practice circles. The ground-level community where Tier A and B development happens and where practitioners become known through practice.

Intentional Society — Relational craft and adult development. The container for T-Group, Circling, and interpersonal range work at Tier B.

CrowdWork DC — Practitioner pipeline and dual-use platform. Bridges conventional and cooperative work contexts.

Inquiring Systems Institute — Fiscal sponsor and 501(c)(3) structure. Provides legal, accounting, HR, and administrative infrastructure while keeping Limicelia mission-accountable and grant-eligible.
