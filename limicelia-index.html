Doc 05 · Interactive Tools

Pricing Tools &
Rate Intelligence

limicelia-pricing-tools.html · Companion to Doc 04

Interactive companion to the Budget Intelligence doc. Five live bar charts (hover for details), a full budget calculator across four pricing models (retainer, fixed fee, hourly, outcome-based), a market comparison chart with 18 named firms, a filterable market rate table, and a sortable role rolodex covering 24 practitioner roles with low/mid/high rate ranges.

All charts respond to filters by tier and engagement type. Calculator outputs include total fee, monthly payments, practitioner hours, effective hourly rate, and percentage of client operating budget.
